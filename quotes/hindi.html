"Embrace the Warmth: Celebrating the Festival of Lohri"

The article provides translations of popular quotes associated with the Indian festival of Lohri, emphasizing the traditions of burning wood, eating peanuts and jaggery, and celebrating happiness. The quotes also convey wishes for the Lohri fire to burn away sorrows and bring light and joy into one's life.

Quote Translation
"लोहड़ी की लक्कड़, खूब जलाओ; मूंगफली, रेवड़ी, खूब खाओ!" "Burn the wood of Lohri; Eat plenty of peanuts and jaggery!"
"लोहड़ी का पर्व है आया, खुशियां मनाने का त्योहार लाया!" "The festival of Lohri has come, it has brought the festival to celebrate happiness!"
"लोहड़ी की आग में दहन हो जाएं सारे गम, खुशियां आएं आपके जीवन में हरदम।" "May all sorrows burn in the fire of Lohri, may happiness come into your life always."
"लोहड़ी का प्रकाश, आपकी जिंदगी को प्रकाशमय बना दे।" "The light of Lohri, may it illuminate your life."
