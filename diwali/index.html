"Unveiling Diwali's Moral & Celebrating with Punjabi Wishes"

The article discusses the moral of the Diwali story, emphasizing the triumph of truth over evil, light over darkness, and good over bad, and the importance of upholding righteousness, truth, and duty. It also provides a list of Diwali wishes in Punjabi, expressing hopes for happiness, fulfilled desires, and the removal of all sorrows.

Moral of Diwali Story
The moral of the Diwali story is that truth always triumphs over evil, light overcomes darkness, and good always prevails over bad. It teaches us to uphold values of righteousness, truth, and duty, even in the face of adversity.

Famous Ramsetu Bridge and Story
Diwali Story discusses the story of Ramsetu stones, which are believed to have been used by Lord Rama to build a bridge to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana. The article also mentions the depiction of this story in movies and TV series, the political debate surrounding the preservation of Ramsetu, and the scientific debate about whether it is a man-made bridge or a natural formation. Ramsetu played a crucial role in the victory of Lord Rama in the epic Ramayana.
