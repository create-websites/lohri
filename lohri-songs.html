Tera Kaun Bichara, Dulha Bhatti walla

Tera kaun bichara ho! Dullah bhatti walla ho!
Dullhe di dhee vyayae hoe!
 Ser shakkar payee ho!
Kudi da laal pathaka hoe!  Kudi da saalu paatta hoe!
Salu kaun samete!
Chache choori kutti! zamidara lutti!
Zamindaar sudhaye!  bade bhole aaye!
Ek bhola reh gaya!
SSipahee pakad ke lai gaya!
 Sipahee ne mari eet!
Sanoo de de lohri te teri jeeve jodi! (Cry or howl!)
Paheenve ro te phannve pit! ”
