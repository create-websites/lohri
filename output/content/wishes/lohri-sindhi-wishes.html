Celebrate the festival of warmth with sweet Sindhi greetings. Explore wishes in Roman Sindhi, traditional script, and poetic verses to share with loved ones.

Dil Je Gehray Saan Wadhayoon

(Heartfelt Congratulations)
Sindhi wishes carry a unique sweetness and warmth. Whether you need a simple one-liner for WhatsApp or a poetic verse for a card, we have curated the best for you.

Easy to Read

Roman Sindhi Wishes

Lohri ji lakh lakh wadhayoon!

Lohri jo din tawhan laaye khushiyoon khanday aahey.

Baah ji roshni saan zindagi roshan thi wayi.

Lohri mubarak haje!

Hee Lohri tawhan ja dukh dard saari chhaday.

Ghar mein sukoon, sukh ar khushhali aahey.

Lohri ji baah saan nayi umeed paida thi.

Tawhan ji kismat hamesha chamkandi rahe.

Lohri ji dil je gehray wadhayoon.

Baah ji garmee jyaan rishtan mein mohabbat wadhay.

Lohri tawhan khe sehat ar sukoon de.

Lohri jo tyohar mubarak haje!

Hee pyaro tyohar dilan khe qareeb anay.

Lohri tawhan ji sab khwahishoon poori kare.

Lohri mubarak, khushiyoon mubarak!

Traditional Text

Sindhi Script Wishes

لوهڙي جي لک لک واڌايون!

لوهڙي جو ڏهاڙو اوهان لاءِ خوشيون کڻي اچي.

باهه جي روشني سان اوهان جي زندگي روشن ٿئي.

لوهڙي مبارڪ هجي!

هي لوهڙي اوهان جا ڏک درد ساڙي ڇڏي.

گهر ۾ سڪون، سڪرات ۽ خوشحالي اچي.

اوهان جي قسمت سدائين چمڪندي رهي.

لوهڙي جي دلين گهريون واڌايون.

باهه جي گرمي وانگر رشتن ۾ محبت وڌي.

لوهڙي جو تہوار مبارڪ هجي!

اوهان جي گهر ۾ هميشه خوشيون وسن.

هي پيارو تہوار دلين کي ويجهو آڻي.

Rhythmic & Sweet

Poetic & Folk Wishes

Poetic Verses (Roman)

"Baah jale, ujaro thiye,
Dukh saara raakh thiye. Lohri Mubarak!"

"Lohri jo din aayo aa,
Khushiyoon jo paighaam layo aa."

"Til gur ji mithaas saan,
Rishtey bharje pyaar saan."

Folk-Tone Verses

"Lohri aayi, baah jagaayi,
Har mann mein aas samaayi."

"Til gur khaao, geet gaao,
Lohri saan khushiyoon manaao."

"Baah je phere, sukh je dere,
Lohri laay changa sawere."

Short One-Liners (Status Friendly)

Baah jale, zindagi khile. Lohri aayi, umeed laayi. Til gur saan, mithaas har shaan. Baah saan gham jale. Dil roshan, baah sang. Lohri Mubarak, dil saan!
