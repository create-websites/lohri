Lohri Melodies
Heartbeat of Punjab

Songs of Fire & Folklore

Lohri songs are an oral archive of history, preserving the legend of Dulla Bhatti and celebrating community, bravery, and gratitude through simple, powerful verses.

More Than Just Melodies

Sung around the bonfire or while collecting Lohri from homes, these songs connect generations to their roots, teaching values of courage, generosity, and faith.

History

Honoring folk heroes like Dulla Bhatti who fought for justice.

Harvest

Expressing gratitude to nature for fertility and abundance.

Blessings

Showering love and good wishes on newborns and newlyweds.

Legendary Verses

The Most Famous Songs

1. Sundar Mundariye Ho

ਸੁੰਦਰ ਮੁੰਦਰਿਏ ਹੋ!
ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਵਾਲਾ ਹੋ!
ਦੁੱਲੇ ਦੀ ਧੀ ਵਿਆਹੀ ਹੋ!

Translation:

"O beautiful girl Sundri! Who is there to care for you? Dulla Bhatti is your protector! He married off his daughter with honor..."

Significance: Honors Dulla Bhatti’s role in protecting orphan girls. Invokes blessings for protection and social responsibility.

2. Dulla Bhatti Ho

ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਹੋ!
ਸ਼ੇਰ ਪੰਜਾਬ ਦਾ ਹੋ!
ਧਨੀਆਂ ਨੂੰ ਲੁੱਟਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਗਰੀਬਾਂ ਨੂੰ ਦਿੱਤਾ ਹੋ!

Translation:

"O Dulla Bhatti! Lion of Punjab! He looted the rich and gave to the poor!"

Significance: Celebrates resistance against oppression. Keeps alive the spirit of courage and justice found in Punjabi folklore.

3. Ho Tera Kaun Vichara Ho

ਹੋ ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਅੰਬਰਾਂ ਤੋਂ ਉਤਰਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਰੱਬ ਨੇ ਭੇਜਿਆ ਹੋ!

Meaning:

"Who is there to protect you? Sent down from the heavens, God has sent a guardian."

Significance: Reflects faith in divine protection and hope, especially relevant during harsh winters when survival depended on nature.

Nature & Play

Gratitude & Joy

Harvest Gratitude

ਆਦਿ ਆਉਣੀ ਲੋਹੜੀ ਵੇ,
ਵੇਖੋ ਜੱਟਾਂ ਦੀ ਜੋੜੀ ਵੇ...

"Lohri has arrived, Look at the unity of the farmers, Offer sesame, eat sweets..."

Expresses hope for prosperity and thanks nature for the harvest.

Children's Collection

ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ, ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ,
ਜੀਵਣ ਜਗਾਇਆ, ਫਸਲ ਲਿਆਈ...

"Lohri has come, Bringing life and harvest, Give money, sesame, and sweets, Or the fire will tease you!"

Lighthearted verses promoting sharing, generosity, and festive fun.

Songs for New Beginnings

Connecting Lohri with fertility, family continuity, and the celebration of life.

ਪੁੱਤ ਜੰਮਿਆ ਘਰ ਖੁਸ਼ੀ ਆਈ,
ਲੋਹੜੀ ਦੀ ਅੱਗ ਰੌਸ਼ਨਾਈ
ਲੰਮੀ ਉਮਰ, ਚੜ੍ਹਦੀ ਕਲਾ,
ਵੱਸਦਾ ਰਹੇ ਇਹ ਘਰ ਸਦਾ

"A son is born, happiness fills the home, The Lohri fire shines bright, May life be long and spirits high, May this home always prosper."
