Tradition of Heena

Henna


Mehandi has its own meaning in India and the Middle East. It's like a tradition

in India, women used to decorate their hands and feet with Henna Mehndi. Henna

Mehandi is basically a paste made from the leaves of the henna plant. Henna Mehandi considered a symbol of happiness for

married women in Indian culture. Here is a belief in India that women with

mehendi has left a dark color will be a lot of love and care of her husband.
