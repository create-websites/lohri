The Essential Guide

Lohri celebrates warmth, harvest, and community. Use this interactive checklist to ensure a meaningful celebration, from preparation to the final bonfire prayers.

Celebrate with Purpose

This guide covers every step of the evening, ensuring you honor traditions while celebrating the spirit of gratitude and togetherness.

Prepare

Gather items like Til, Gur, and wood before sunset.

Offer

Perform Agni Arpan and Parikrama with devotion.

Celebrate

Sing songs, share Prasad, and enjoy the feast.
